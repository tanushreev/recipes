Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup sooji/semolina
- 1 chopped onion
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
- 1/2 tsp urad dal
- 1/2 grated carrot
- 1/4 cup peas
- 1 1/2 cups water
- maggi tastemaker (optional)
- 1/4 tsp hing
- salt 
- lemon juice 

Directions:
1. Heat 1 tbsp oil.
2. Add mustard seeds and allow to pop.
3. Add approx. 1/2 tsp urad daal.
4. Add a pinch of hing, 1/2 tsp maggi tastemaker.
5. Mix well and cook for 10 seconds.
6. Add 1 chopped onion, 1/2 grated carrot, handful peas.
7. Cook till onion turns translucent.
8. Add sooji and 3 times water.
9. Add salt.
10. Cook on sim gas for few minutes.
11. Add lemon if u like.
12. Serve hot.
